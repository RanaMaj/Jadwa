    About us

    Jadwa Company

    Jadwa is an institution, was founded in April 2012 and located in Gaza City, Palestine, that specializes in economic consulting and entrepreneurship. Jadwa focus on improving the efficiency of sectors of the economy through planned and built interventions based on accurate analysis of the internal business environment as well as factors influencing the unstable environment.
